Step On

Date: 12/26/2020
Medium: Assemblage / Sculpting / Form board
Size: 11.8*18.5*13.7 inch / 30*47*35cm


Description:
Many of my experiences serve as stepping stones for me to reach my goals. Through assemblage, I created a sculpture symbolizing these experiences and my aspirations. The mountain represents my journey, while a star atop it signifies my ultimate goal. This artwork encapsulates my desires and growth.
