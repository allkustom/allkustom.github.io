Safe-Crosswalk

Minister of Culture and Sports Award
Date: 12/25/2020
Medium: 3D Modeling / Photoshop
Prototype Size: 26*20*105 cm


Download Proposal File (Korean)


Description:
1st place at 'Design Gyeonggi' competition. Designing public facilities for the safety of the elderly was a key topic. Based on the 7 Universal Design Principles, it solved the problems faced by users and introduced a design that could be friendly to the public. After qualifying, he was elected as the winner through a competitive presentation for the finals.
