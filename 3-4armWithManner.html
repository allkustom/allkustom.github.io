Arm With Manners

Date: 12/15/2020
Medium: Kuka / Performance
Size: 393*393 inch(performance stage), 4min 17sec
Description:
As the final task of the Seoul Institute of the Arts, Robotic art lecture, the performance of acting with the robot arm Kuka was constructed. I was with Seongrok Jeong, Rigeon Jeong, and Junha Hwang, and as a team leader, I was in charge of deriving concepts, organizing performances, programming for Kuka's movements, editing music for performances, and playing the main character. It is a waste to express a good material called Kuka as installation art, so we made a lot of effort to show Kuka and the actor breathing together. The main story is that a person who has used Kuka to replace one arm goes to the movie theater. Kuka suddenly malfunctions, and the story unfolds through conflicts with other audiences. And the performance was completed through a twist.
