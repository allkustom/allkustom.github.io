My fist Unity & Blender Project
(100% handmade assets, except music)


Click here to download the game



Description:
'Did I live a life without regrets?'
This story-driven game focuses on an imperfect time-machine that stores a man's memories. Although it cannot rewind the course of his life, it can rewrite the speed of his memories. Happy and pleasant memories race by in an instant, where an hour feels like a minute. However, dull and sorrowful moments drag on endlessly, turning a minute into an hour. Which memories hold more value? By insering his memories into the time module, you can encapsulate the essence of his life. Is it possible to live a life without regrets? It's up to you.
Game control:
Move: Wasd
Dash: Shift
Interact: Mouse left-click(with highlighted object)

Language:
