Pistol Laser Sight

Date: 03/25/2023
Medium: 3D printing / Circuit
Size: 6*2*4 inch / 15*5*10 cm


Description:
For the movie name 'Freelancer', the director, Max Hahn, requested me to make a laser sight that fits to the prop pistol. Making a simple circuit that able to turn on the laser pointer. The size and the finishing through the electric tape, it show the aesthetic of cyberpunk.
