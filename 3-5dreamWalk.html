Dream walk

Date: 11/18/2022
Medium: P5.js
Size: 600*400 pixels


Click here to play the game(fullscreen)


Click here to see code


Description:
My first interactive game based on P5.js. I enjoyed playing the Legend of Zelda game series very much as a child, and I'm still a huge fan. In particular, some of the puzzles featured in the "Legend of Zelda" series were still memorable. The sleep disorder I was experiencing at the time was used as a story to express the anxiety I experienced. In addition, puzzles were placed in the appropriate place to proceed with the story so that the player could understand the story while solving the problem. Through this work, I was able to understand the principle and implementation method of the system.
