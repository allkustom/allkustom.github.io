DJ Reactive Visual Projection

Networking Party

Date: 07/19/24
Medium: After Effect / Illustrator / Madmapper / Touchdesigner
Size: 29000*1200 pixel, 120min


Description:
Used Touchdesigner for creating the audio reactive visual that provides the immersive experience during the DJ party time to the audience.
There was a technical issue that unable to run Touchdesigner and Madmapper at the same PC because of the leak of graphic performance. I spent 2 weeks to optimize the system
I used NDI video transporting system through the local network between Madmapper Window PC and Touchdesigner calculating laptop. Allocating the graphic calculating to the additional laptop provided the non-delay video play environment. That solution has a possibility to provide the complex graphic calculating visual with any system.
