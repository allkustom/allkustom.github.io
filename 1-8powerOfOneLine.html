Power of One-Line

Exhibition Documentary
Date: 11/22/2019
Medium: After Effect / Photoshop /Premiere
Size: 3840*2160 pixel, 2min 54sec
Language: Korean


Description:
At Power of One-Line Exhibition, I was the head of the video record department and produced a documentary. Various artists gathered to convey the power of Korean were interviewed to record the contents of the work. And this documentary contains the process of the exhibition, and the reactions of the audience who participated in the exhibition.

The exhibition was held on Nov.30th ~ Dec.6th at National Hanguel Museum Annex. Sponsored by Korean Paper and Seven-brau
