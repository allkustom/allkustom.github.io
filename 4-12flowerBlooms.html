A Flower Blooming In The Mud

Date: 12/22/2021
Medium: Sculpting / Epoxy resin / soil
Size: 6*6*6 inch / 15*15*15 cm


Description:
Lotus is the only flower that blooms in the mud. To me, mud feels like a harsh restraint. I am also living in a world under a lot of restraint, and I always tried to overcome it and achieve a complete self to look at the world. I used Epoxy resin and soil to capture a fragment of a muddy pond. And I used sculping to create my appearance of accepting the world by being born from lotus flowers.
