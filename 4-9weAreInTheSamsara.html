We Are In The Samsara

Date: 11/26/2021
Medium: Wood board / 3D printing / Bearing / Digital drawing
Size: 55*5*22.5 inch / 140*13*57 cm


Description:
This work embodies the concept of Samsara (Buddhist reincarnation). The quality of the next life is determined by the weight of the sins committed in current life, so I strive to live properly. I created an infinite looping image for depict the aspect of Samsara. To make a looping image system, I used 3D printer for making gears and frameworks, and combined it with industrial bearings.
