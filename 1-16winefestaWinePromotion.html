Wine Promotion Projection

Highball&Wine Festa

Date: 08/02/24 ~ 08/04/24
Medium: After Effect / Illustrator / Madmapper / Touchdesigner
Size: 7000*1200 pixel, 6min


Description:
This is a promotion video that shows the wine from the wine seller who attended the festival The purpose of this promotion was conveying the information that which wine seller is selling which wine that from the specific region. So, I focused on the grid that fits to the structure and less motion to show the information clearly. By searching and organizing the information of products, I was able to understand the priority of information and setting the criteria to matching the form of informations(ex. Accents on the naem/Region/etc)
