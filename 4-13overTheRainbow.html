Over The Rainbow

Date: 12/9/2021
Medium: Wood board / Nail / Thread / Acrylic paint
Size: 11.6*16.3 inch / 29.5*41.5 cm


Description:
Though we see rainbows, it impossible to touch them, much like our ideal dreams. This work illustrates my aspirations, by drawing my hand in a space that cannot be reached. In the future, I hope to truly touch the rainbow.
