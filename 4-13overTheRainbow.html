Over The Rainbow

Date: 12/9/2021
Medium: Wood board / Nail / Thread / Acrylic paint
Size: 11.6*16.3 inch / 29.5*41.5 cm


Description:
Many people think of it as a wish that cannot reach the rainbow. I also don't think I'm ready to go beyond the rainbow yet. I was definitely in a different space from the rainbow, so I tried to place my hands trying to hold the rainbow in a space where we could not meet each other. In the process, I wondered if I could express shading with threads, and I tried to draw my hands using threads and nails. In that way, I succeeded in drawing my hand in a space that could not reach the rainbow painted on the wooden board. In the future, I will finally reach the rainbow by reducing the space gap.
